Applie pie


Total Time

Prep: 20 min. Bake: 45 min.


Makes

8 servings


Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup sugar

  2. 1/2 cup packed brown sugar

  3. 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

  4. 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

  5. 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

  6. 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg

  7. 6 to 7 cups thinly sliced peeled tart apples

  8. 1 tablespoon lemon juice

  9. Pastry for double-crust pie (9 inches)

  10. 1 tablespoon butter

  11. 1 large egg white

  12. Additional sugar


Directions

In a small bowl, combine the sugars, flour and spices; set aside. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat.
Line a 9-in. pie plate with bottom crust; trim even with edge. Fill with apple mixture; dot with butter. Roll remaining crust to fit top
of pie; place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edges. Cut slits in crust. Beat egg white until foamy; brush over crust. Sprinkle with sugar.
Cover edges loosely with foil. Bake at 375Â° for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly, 20-25 minutes longer.
Cool on a wire rack.


Nutrition Facts

1 piece: 414 calories, 16g fat (7g saturated fat), 14mg cholesterol, 227mg sodium, 67g carbohydrate (38g sugars, 2g fiber), 3g protein.
